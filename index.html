Company Overview

Nishi Plastic Industries is a reputable company established in 1995, with its factory situated in Silvassa, Dadra and Nagar Haveli. Our company is a renowned manufacturer of PP box strapping rolls. With years of experience we have established ourselves as a leader in the industry. Our commitment to quality, innovation and customer satisfaction sets us apart. Our product range includes a variety of sizes, colours, and strengths to meet diverse packaging needs. We are also involved in the wholesaling and trading of Adhesive Tapes and Jumbo Rolls. Our prime focus is to offer a comprehensive packaging solution to our valued customers.

Over the years, we have witnessed substantial growth, earning a commendable reputation in the market. Our dedication to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a reliable name in the packaging industry. With a wide range of products and a strong commitment to meeting market demands, we continue to excel in our endeavours to provide superior packaging solutions

Our Products

At our Company, we take pride in our excellent quality PP box strapping rolls. Our products are manufactured using premium grade raw materials and advanced technology. Our products are known for their high tensile strength, durability and resistance to breakage. Our range of strapping rolls caters to various industries, including logistics, packaging, and manufacturing.

PP box strapping offers numerous advantages for various industries. Its high tensile strength provides maximum security for packages, preventing shifting and damage during transportation. It is resistant to moisture, UV rays, and chemicals, ensuring the durability of packaged goods. PP box strapping is also cost-effective, lightweight, and easy to handle, making it an ideal choice for efficient and reliable packaging solutions.

We achieve excellence in the PP box strapping industry by following the best practices, investing in advanced technology, maintaining quality control, optimizing packaging and supply chain management, and providing exceptional customer service, We are committed and understand that continuous improvement and adaptation are the key in this dynamic industry
